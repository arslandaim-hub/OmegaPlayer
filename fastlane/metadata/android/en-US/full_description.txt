OmegaPlayer is a multimedia player for Android. It combines a high-performance video engine with a sophisticated music player and a secure private vault to protect your personal media. 
Application has no internet connectivity at all, everything is ad free and no trackers whatsoever.

Key Features:

SILKY SMOOTH VIDEO PLAYBACK
  Optimized for large files: Open 4K and Ultra HD videos without lag.
  Advanced Buffering: Zero-stutter playback.
  Gesture Controls: Swipe left for brightness, right for volume, and horizontal to seek.
  Background Play: Keep listening to your videos even when the screen is off.

 PREMIUM AUDIO EXPERIENCE
  High-Fidelity Playback: Supports all popular audio formats (MP3, FLAC, WAV, etc.).
  Folder-Based Navigation. Dynamic Album Art: Adapts background based audio thumbnail color.
  Smart Controls: Repeat modes, skip, and smooth seek bars.

 PRIVATE MEDIA VAULT
  PIN & Biometric Protection: App supports both fingerprint and pin support for better security and user ease.

 MODERN & RESPONSIVE UI
  Material 3 Design: A clean, beautiful interface that supports Light and Dark modes.
  Optimized Performance: Lightweight and battery-efficient architecture.
